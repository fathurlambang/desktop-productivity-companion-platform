# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Software Engineers, UI Designers, Students, and Freelancers who need help staying focused, managing tasks, and remembering meetings while working on their desktop.

## Product Purpose
A lightweight, offline-first desktop productivity companion app that combines a Pomodoro timer, task management, meeting reminders, and a non-intrusive on-screen "Desktop Companion" character to provide visual feedback and help users maintain focus.

## Brand Personality
Focused, minimal, companionable, responsive, offline-first.

## Anti-references
Heavy, distracting, online-dependent SaaS apps. Complex project management tools with steep learning curves. Overly gamified, demanding, or noisy apps.

## Design Principles
- Minimal UI: Simple, uncluttered, and easy to use.
- Lightweight & Fast: Minimal resource consumption and zero configuration.
- Privacy First: Local-only, no telemetry or account required.
- Unobtrusive Presence: The companion provides feedback without breaking focus.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for readability, particularly with the pastel theme.
- Clear visual hierarchy.
- Support for keyboard navigation and standard desktop accessibility features.
